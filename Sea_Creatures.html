Stingray

Stingrays can be around 14 inches in length and some stingrays can weigh up to 3,000 pounds. The stingrays diet includes snails,clams, and shrimp, they like to eat fairly small creatuers.

Eel

Eels can grow to be around 2 inches or up to 13ft. Adult eels can weigh up to 55 pounds. Eel usually eat fish but some eel eat cephalopods such as squid and octopus.

Oarfish

The orafish can grow up to 3,000 feet long, although they don't really come up to the surface. They usually live in the deep part of the sea. Orafish feed on zooplankton and shrimp.
